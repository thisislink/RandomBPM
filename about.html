About Random BPM

The developer of this small project is also a musician and was looking for a way to make her songwriting process faster, more challenging, but also more efficient.

This meant removing the decision making process for:

  • Choosing the BPM (beats per minute)
  • Choosing the time signature
  • Choosing the key signature

GarageBand was her main DAW (digital audio workstation) on iOS, which has limitions on the min and max BPM and time signature options.

So, keeping the songwriting requirements and DAW limitations in mind, RandomBPM was born.
